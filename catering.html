Vegetarian House Catering can accommodate any workplace function or private event you may have, be it power lunch boxes to a wedding banquet!

Our aim is  to make your dining experience pleasurable and worry free. We will work with you on your menu selection, using our expertise to ensure that your dishes are suited to your guests palate.Our innovative chefs can create a menu that will please your vegan and non-vegetarian guests, using non GMO ingredients with a variety of International flavors and textures.

Most corporate luncheons can be organized in as little as 24 hours. Please call us for more details.
